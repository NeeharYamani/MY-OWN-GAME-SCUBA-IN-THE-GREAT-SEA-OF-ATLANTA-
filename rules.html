Rules Of This Game!

There are 3 Levels in this Game

1: Use UP and DOWN Arrow Keys to Move

2: Avoid the Fishes and Other Sea Creatures

3: Collect all the ancient pieces and the treasure!

4: Click on the Reset image to restart the game in LEVEL 1 (if u touch the fishes and other creatures)

5. It does not matter even if you lose. You have to try your best!




Story

You are the Scuba Diver who is on a search of antique pieces and treasure in the great Sea of ATLANTA!

But, there are some fishes and sea creatures who are protecting the antique pieces and the treasure. Do not Touch Them!
